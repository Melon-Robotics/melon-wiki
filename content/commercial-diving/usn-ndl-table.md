## How to Use These Tables

### Single Dive (no prior dives within 12 hours)

1. Find your planned depth in the **Depth** column
2. Read across to find the **No-Stop Limit** — your maximum bottom time
3. Find the column matching your actual bottom time to read your **Repetitive Group** on surfacing
4. If your planned bottom time exceeds the No-Stop Limit, decompression stops are required — use [Table 9-9](/commercial-diving/usn-air-dive-tables/)

### Repetitive Dive (prior dive within 12 hours)

1. Note your **Repetitive Group** from the previous dive (Table 9-7)
2. Find your surface interval in **Table 9-8 Part 1** to determine your new group
3. Use **Table 9-8 Part 2** with your new group and next dive depth → **Residual Nitrogen Time (RNT)**
4. Add RNT to planned bottom time → **Equivalent Single Dive Time (ESDT)**
5. If ESDT exceeds the NDL, use Table 9-9 for the required decompression schedule
